## Decisions

- Gyro Hub is the Project Hub, not only a dashboard.
- Dashboard is a display layer, not a source of truth.
- `dashboard.json` is the common data layer for future automation.
- `data/` will hold structured machine-readable project data.
- Weekly reports will record the flow of the project over time.
- Japanese users should be able to understand the full overview through `README_jp.md`.
